About Us

32 YEARS IN THE TRADE.

The Corner Box is the digital archive of Legends of Superheros, a staple of the New England collecting community since 1994.

In an era of algorithm-driven pricing and faceless flipping, we believe in Provenance.

We don't use stock photos. We don't guess at grades. We treat every Silver Age key and Modern variant as a piece of history that deserves to be preserved for the next generation.

Welcome to the Archive.
